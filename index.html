About Us

Etilweni PTY LTD is a South African based Consulting Engineering firm, which was established in 2011. We have a diverse portfolio of Professional Services with the aim of contributing towards the sustainable infrastructure development in South Africa and beyond.

As a multi-disciplinary empowerment company, we fuel African-led collaborations, economic growth and high returns for our partners. Most importantly, our strategic imperative is to continuously provide sustainable and life-improving environments for the people we impact.

Our Vision

To successfully oversee our clients’ projects from planning, design phase to project implementation stages through collaboration that inspires revenue and business growth – that’s what makes us Pioneers of Evolution.

Our Suite of Expertise

 

Civil & Structural Engineering

The range of civil and structural engineering services include: water and sanitation; bridge and rail engineering; roads and stormwater; and structural buildings.
 

Road, Traffic & Transportation

Our Road, traffic and transportation engineering discipline branches from the civil and structural engineering department. This involves the planning, design, construction, operation, and maintenance of roads, bridges, and tunnels to ensure safe and effective transportation of people and goods.
 

Town & Regional Planning

Our Town and Regional planners plan and develop the infrastructure of towns and cities to improve spatial and social imbalances in urban and rural areas across the country. Our approach is functional in conjunction with the following services: land surveying, environmental impact assessment and geotechnical engineering.


 

Mechancial & Electrical Engineering

Our seasoned staff of skilled service engineers are experienced in all areas including planning, analysing, designing, optimising, installation, commissioning and computerised control systems.


 

Quantity Surveying

Etilweni PTY Ltd has experienced Quantity Surveyors who assist clients to make informed decisions regarding cost efficiency of the construction projects.


 

Project & Program Management

Etilweni PTY Ltd offers a range of project and programme management capabilities that ensure that projects remain on course and on schedule.


 

Architectural & Draughting Services

The company has in-house qualified and experienced architects who have completed a range of plans and drawings for different projects.


 

Procurement & Supply

Etilweni PTY Ltd has adopted a procurement policy that the company uses to source material and equipment. Sectors include: mining; rail; manufacturing & construction.


 

Property & Real Estate

Our Real Estate Portfolio encampasses property development activities ranging from buying and renovation, re-lease and selling of existing buildings to the purchase of raw land and the sale of developed land.
