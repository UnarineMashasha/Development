    A Word From Our CEO

    To our Clients, Associates, Friends, and the Etilweni family. I greet you all in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ. It gives me great pleasure to be part of a team that is grounded in excellence, and client service and that understands our changing economic climate. As our ‘post-Covid’ recovery continues to unfold, more fundamental questions arise: What comes now? What parts of our business need to be accelerated to tackle the future? And, what does the new big picture look like? To address and answer these questions, our focus is recognising that we are now faced with new but exciting challenges to ensure social and economic growth. And, to do this, we have to concentrate on strong partnerships, technological innovation, social investment, and agile responsiveness to our current post-Covid environment. Our presence on the continent has seen increased population growth and urbanisation, therefore, the critical need for infrastructure development remains. However, the rate of executing existing service delivery such as electricity, water, sanitation, access roads, and adequate housing cannot keep up with the demand. This is why we endeavor to develop the unique solutions required to provide much-needed economic and social infrastructure in Africa. I believe that nothing grows in isolation, therefore our priority is ensuring that our work is strongly aligned with government priorities, and we see public-private partnerships as an essential mechanism to design, build and finance successful projects. We also understand that technology in engineering is the cornerstone of addressing industry challenges. I believe that as an industry we need to continue to drive technological innovation and stay well-informed of the latest trends in digital modernisation to continue to shape the future of the sector. In addition to the fluid economic situation, the industry also faces severe challenges related to skills shortages and social issues. It is, therefore, a pleasure to be part of an organisation with growing corporate social investment initiatives designed to focus on mentorship, youth, and community development. To our clients, thank you for partnering with us. Led by experts, we are a team ready to identify key priorities and use our experience to influence solutions that will leave a positive legacy in the future. Best regards, Jabulani Ntimani.
