Corporate Social Responsibility

  • Home
  • CSR
Etilweni continuously partners with local communities and government to address social challenges and facilitate sustained economic growth. We have dedicated ourselves to creating a platform for community development with every project we acquire. Over the years, we have partnered with local organisations to sponsor various development initiatives. Through our work, we are committed to actively participating in bringing solutions to the challenges that our communities face in line with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Etilweni Covid-19 Response

In response to the Covid-19 pandemic that affected the global community, Etilweni partnered amongst others with the following institutions to distribute food parcels; Kings Covenant Christian Centre in Naturena, Greater Giyani Municipality, and DRT Municipality. We donated over 500 food parcels and we are grateful to all the institutions that allowed us to partner with them.
